Let's Feast Together

Let's Feast Together is a website application designed to help with meal planning for individuals and families. It helps users find recipes, organize weekly or monthly meal plans, generate shopping lists, and save favorite meals in a file. This solves the common problem of juggling recipes from multiple sources and forgetting ingredients when shopping. My motivation is to create a user-friendly platform that encourages cooking healthy meals at home.
